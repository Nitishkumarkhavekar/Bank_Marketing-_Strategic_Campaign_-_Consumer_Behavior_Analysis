# 🏦 Bank Marketing: Strategic Campaign & Consumer Behavior Analysis

## 👋 Overview

This project focuses on identifying the key drivers behind customer subscriptions to term deposits. Using a dataset of bank marketing campaigns, I performed an end-to-end analysis to determine which customer segments (demographics, job types, and financial status) are most likely to respond to marketing efforts and how contact strategies impact conversion rates.

## 🚀 Key Features & Analysis

  * **Customer Segmentation:** Created Age Groups and Customer Segments to identify high-conversion niches like students and retirees.
  * **Campaign Efficiency:** Evaluated the impact of call duration and contact frequency on subscription success.
  * **Behavioral Analysis:** Analyzed how previous campaign outcomes influence current customer decisions.
  * **Channel Optimization:** Compared the performance of cellular vs. telephone contact methods.
  * **Timing Study:** Identified the most effective months and quarters for launching marketing drives.

## 🛠️ Excel Toolkit Used

  * **Data Engineering:** Cleaned raw campaign data, standardized categorical values, and handled class imbalances.
  * **Advanced Formulas:** Converted call durations to minutes and created contact frequency "buckets" using logical functions.
  * **Aggregation:** Leveraged **Pivot Tables** to calculate conversion rates across various demographic filters.
  * **Feature Engineering:** Derived response indicators and timing features (Month/Quarter) from raw timestamp data.
  * **Visualization:** Developed professional charts to visualize the "Success Rate vs. Call Duration" trend.

## ⚙️ How It Works

1.  **Phase 1: Data Pre-processing** – Cleaned the bank dataset, ensuring consistency between customer profiles and campaign interactions.
2.  **Phase 2: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** – Used descriptive statistics to find the "average" customer profile that subscribes.
3.  **Phase 3: Deep-Dive Analysis** – Investigated the "Law of Diminishing Returns" regarding repeated follow-up calls.
4.  **Phase 4: Strategic Recommendations** – Drafted a final report with 5 actionable steps to improve the bank's marketing ROI.

## 📈 Key Business Insights

  * **High-Response Segments:** Retired individuals and students showed significantly higher response rates compared to the middle-aged working class.
  * **The "Golden Window":** Analysis proved that longer, meaningful calls (quality over quantity) were the strongest predictor of a "Yes" response.
  * **Contact Decay:** Found that after the 3rd or 4th contact attempt, the probability of subscription drops drastically, suggesting a waste of marketing resources.
  * **Top Channel:** Cellular communication outperformed traditional landlines by a wide margin in terms of successful conversions.
